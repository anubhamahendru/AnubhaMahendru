About the Artist

Anubha is born and brought up in New Delhi, India. While pursuing her academic and professional interests in History and Journalism, she simultaneously nurtured her passion for art and photography, and has been creating bespoke commissioned paintings since 2018. She expresses herself through the medium of abstract art, and also specialises in customised paintings modelled on references provided by her clients.
